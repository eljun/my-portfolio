The Spirit & Flesh

Romans 7:4-6

4 Wherefore, my brethren, ye also are become dead to the law by the body of Christ; that ye should be married to another, even to him who is raised from the dead, that we should bring forth fruit unto God. 5 For when we were in the flesh, the motions of sins, which were by the law, did work in our members to bring forth fruit unto death. 6 But now we are delivered from the law, that being dead wherein we were held; that we should serve in newness of spirit, and not in the oldness of the letter.
  • The two nature of man Spirit & Carnal (flesh)
  • Spirit and Carnal are constant battle until they end Carnal mind cannot get us to heaven

Romans 8:6-8

"6The mind of the flesh is death, but the mind of the Spirit is life and peace, 7because the mind of the flesh is hostile to God: It does not submit to God’s Law, nor can it do so. 8Those controlled by the flesh cannot please God."

  • Saul saves the best for God (1 Samuel 15:15). God wanted Amalekites dead, down to the last donkey. Saud had a better idea. Bye bye house of Saul..
  • God wants the early Christian to worship Him alone, but they sought worship idols, these resulted into widespread apostasy.

14 But the natural man receiveth not the things of the Spirit of God: for they are foolishness unto him: neither can he know them, because they are spiritually discerned.

But if the person has the spirit and has the hunger after righteousness, the spirit will guide him and reveal to him that things that a carnal mind cannot see.

Romans 7:14 - "Law is Spiritual, I am carnal"

14 For we know that the law is spiritual: but I am carnal, sold under sin.

  • You might not kill someone according to the letter of the law, but you have the same judgement when you start hating your brother?
  • You might not commit adultery according to the letter of the law, but you commited the same sin when you look at a woman with lust?
  • The letter and the spiritual law are shared.
  • Sin is a like a magnet and it is attracted to carnal things the lush of the flesh.
  • Paul said that there is nothing good in his body..
  • But that doesn't mean that our body is evil or always lead to do evil things...
  • Not all carnal things are good like when you desire a food and a water to drink, it only becomes corrupted when you decided to eat or drink that is not good for your health. That's the reason why God give's us brain?
